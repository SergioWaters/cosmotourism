Blue Origin

suborbital flight

Blue Origin's New Shepard suborbital rocket on July 20, 2021 for the first time in the company's history brought a capsule with people to space height, with the richest man in the world, Jeff Bezos, on board.

The New Shepard, 18 m high and 4 m wide, is a reusable spacecraft. The booster set raises a sealed passenger ship to an altitude of about 76 km. The impulse propels the capsule up into space.

After that, it descends to the ground under three parachutes. The booster is also returning home.
